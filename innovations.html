Innovation #1 — TorqueSim DD18 Direct‑Drive Motor System

The DD18 is powered by a custom‑engineered direct‑drive motor designed to deliver smooth, consistent, and realistic force feedback. Unlike many competitors that rely on off‑the‑shelf motors, TorqueSim’s DD18 uses a reinforced rotor, precision‑machined shaft, and high‑efficiency windings to maximize torque output while keeping noise and heat extremely low.

Key Innovations:

  • 18 Nm peak torque for realistic race‑car steering feel
  • High‑resolution encoder for ultra‑precise steering input
  • Active cooling system to maintain performance during long sessions
  • Modular expansion panel for future add‑ons and customization

This system gives racers the same level of immersion found in professional simulators, but at a fraction of the cost — making high‑end performance accessible to everyone.

Innovation #2 — Electromagnetic Adaptive Pedal System

Traditional sim pedals rely on springs or rubber blocks, which wear out and feel inconsistent. TorqueSim’s pedal set uses a fully electronic, electromagnet‑based resistance system that adapts to the driver in real time. This creates a braking feel that is smoother, more realistic, and far more durable than mechanical systems.

Key Innovations:

  • 6 Nm peak resistance for realistic brake pressure
  • 20 individually controlled electromagnets for customizable pedal feel
  • High‑resolution position encoder for precise input detection
  • Hot‑swappable pedal modules for quick setup changes

This technology allows drivers to fine‑tune their braking force, pedal travel, and resistance curve — giving them a competitive edge and a more immersive driving experience.
