Investment Opportunity

TorqueSim is redefining what high‑performance sim racing hardware can be — and we’re inviting early investors to help shape the future.

The Opportunity

The sim racing market is expanding rapidly as esports grows and home simulation becomes more accessible. Yet most hardware options fall into two extremes: low‑quality budget gear or overpriced premium systems.

TorqueSim fills the gap with engineering‑driven, modular, durable hardware that delivers professional‑grade realism at a fair price. This positions us uniquely in a market hungry for innovation and accessibility.

The Ask

TorqueSim is seeking $10,000 for our first round of funding to help develop our innovations and products, refine manufacturing processes, and support our first production run.

In return, investors receive:

  • Lifetime preferred pricing on all TorqueSim products
  • Early access to prototypes and new releases
  • Advisory input on future product development

Why Invest in TorqueSim?

TorqueSim shines because we focus on engineering, modularity, and long‑term value — not gimmicks or locked ecosystems. Our products are for ordinary people who desire realism, durability, and customization — not paying for luxury brands.

  • Modular, repairable hardware that extends product lifespan
  • Open ecosystem compatible with third‑party wheels and rigs
  • Advanced force feedback for true motorsport realism
  • Strong market demand from sim racers upgrading from entry‑level gear

Use of Funds

Your funds will go towards:

  • Prototyping production‑ready products
  • Small‑scale manufacturing and machinery
  • Electronics and product testing for guaranteed quality
  • Marketing, branding, and building a customer base

Every cent helps build a scalable foundation for TorqueSim.

Our Roadmap

TorqueSim’s development plan includes:

  • Phase 1: Launch of the DD18 direct‑drive wheelbase
  • Phase 2: Release of modular pedal systems and shifters
  • Phase 3: Software suite for telemetry tuning and customization
  • Future: Expansion into wheel rims, quick‑release systems, and accessories

Each phase strengthens TorqueSim’s position as a leader in the evolving sim racing industry.
